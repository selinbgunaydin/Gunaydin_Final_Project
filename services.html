Supporting Our Community

Macon County is dedicated to providing efficient, transparent, and accessible e-government services for all residents.

Our department ensures that residents have 24-hour access to essential county information, legal forms, and administrative resources. By digitizing our workflow, we reduce wait times and improve the quality of life for every citizen in our historic county.

Digital Accessibility

Access tax records, public documents, and building permits from any device, ensuring transparency across the county.

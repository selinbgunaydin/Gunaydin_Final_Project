The County Seal & Heritage

Established in 1828, Macon County carries a rich history in the heart of the Blue Ridge Mountains.

Our official seal is more than just a logo; it is a symbol of our commitment to the residents, businesses, and visitors of this great county. It represents the authority of the Board of Commissioners and the transparency of our local government services.

Founded: 1828 Region: Western North Carolina
